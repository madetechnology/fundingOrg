About Funding Org

We are a dedicated platform focused on empowering schools across New Zealand to access funding derived from the proceeds of Class 4 gambling, as regulated by the Gambling Act 2003. Our mission is to bridge the gap between available community funding and the educational institutions that need it most, ensuring that schools can enhance their resources, programs, and overall student experience.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every school has the tools and knowledge necessary to secure funding for vital projects that benefit students and the wider community. By leveraging technology and data-driven insights, we aim to create a transparent and efficient funding landscape.

What We Offer

Our platform provides a suite of AI-powered tools designed to simplify the funding application process and maximize the impact of grants received. Key features include:

  • Funding Awareness Platform: A comprehensive database of available grants tailored for schools, complete with notifications for new opportunities.
  • Application Assistance Chatbot: An interactive tool that guides users through the grant application process, ensuring all requirements are met.
  • Grant Management System: A cloud-based solution for tracking applications and managing funds effectively, allowing schools to monitor their financial health.
  • Data Analytics Dashboard: Insights into funding trends and community impacts, helping schools tailor their projects for better success rates.
  • Collaboration Network: A space for schools to connect with local organizations, fostering partnerships that enhance funding applications.
  • Training and Workshops: Regular sessions led by experts in grant writing and fundraising strategies, equipping schools with essential skills.

Commitment to Community

We are committed to transparency and accountability in all our operations. By ensuring that schools are informed about how gambling revenues are utilized, we promote responsible funding practices that benefit the entire community.

Join us in our mission to unlock the potential of New Zealand's educational institutions through accessible funding opportunities. Together, we can create a brighter future for our students and communities.
